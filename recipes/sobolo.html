Sobolo

Description.

Roselle Juice locally called sobolo in Ghana is a West African drink which is best enjoyed while chiled.

It's reddish colour and nice aroma makes it one of West African's favorite smoothies.

Ingredients

  • 1400 ml water
  • 1 tbsp ginger cut up in smaller chunks
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 selim
  • 200 grams dried hibiscus flowers
  • sugar

Steps.

  1. Bring water to a boil and add ginger, cloves and selim and leave in for about 2 minutes
  2. Add the hibiscus flowers to the water with spices and boil for another 3 minutes.
  3. Leave the mixture to cool and then strain. Add sugar to taste.
  4. Fill up in bottles and seal. Leave to chill in fridge or store in the freezer if you have made a large batch
